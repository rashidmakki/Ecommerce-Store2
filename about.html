Our Mission


Government has launched a ‘Go Tribal Campaign’ to provide global market to tribal made products. Govt. has also adopted E-Commerce Selling mode to market and sale of innovative products. We are Providing Tribal Community Support by marketing their products and promoting their Talent & Work Globally.

Who we are?


E-Store is an indian electronic commerce company with headquarters in Delhi. It is the largest retailers in India. E-Store starts as an blog, but soon diversified, selling handmade products, electronic gadgets and many more items. E-Store also sells-low end product like USB cables and other accessories. E-Store has a seperate retail websites for India, France, US, China, Japan.

Support


Tribals are known to be the autochthonous people of the land and are the integral segment of society. Marketing Tribal Handicrafts, Jewellery, Clothes and other products at nominal prices, we are providing them a means of business and growth oppurtunities. This will promote their community and their talent globally.
